Activities

According to Indian Constitution, A range of activities and opportunities are offered to prisoners while they are in custody. These can include: employment (e.g. bakery, laundry, kitchen, woodwork shop, tailorshop, farming, gardening, community work, cleaning); education (a range of educational opportunities are provided) programs (including targeted offence-related programs as well as other programs to assist prisoners); sport and recreation (including a range of individual and team-based activities and competitions); hobbies; library (materials and services provided by the State Library of Tasmania).
